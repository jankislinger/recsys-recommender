Should we Embed? A Study on the Online Performance of Utilizing Embeddings for Real-Time Job Recommendations (2019)

Authors: Elisabeth Lex, Emanuel Lacic, Valentin Slawicek, Markus Reiter-Haas, Tomislav Duricic

In this work, we present the findings of an online study, where we explore the impact of utilizing embeddings to recommend job postings under real-time constraints. On the Austrian job platform Studo Jobs, we evaluate two popular recommendation scenarios: (i) providing similar jobs and, (ii) personalizing the job postings that are shown on the homepage. Our results show that for recommending similar jobs, we achieve the best online performance in terms of Click-Through Rate when we employ embeddings based on the most recent interaction.
