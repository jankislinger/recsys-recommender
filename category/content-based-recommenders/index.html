A Recommender System to Tackle Enterprise Collaboration (2016)

Authors: Gilmar Alves de Souza, Gabriel de Souza Pereira Moreira

Collaboration platforms have been available for many years, but they struggle to overcome what is known as the collaboration paradox: people want all the benefits provided by such tools, but they won’t invest their times without some sort of immediate gain. To tackle this problem, we developed an enterprise collaboration platform named Smart Canvas. Smart Canvas is a service that allows people to bring, create and curate content relevant to their organizations. The product’s main goal is to leverage companies knowledge and tear down silos by connecting people, teams, and content. One of the main components of the product is a scalable cloud-based Recommender System, built using techniques like Topic Modeling, Content-Based Recommenders and Graph Analysis. In this demo, Smart Canvas will be presented in web and mobile interfaces, with data from a real customer. We are also going to describe details of the models, algorithms and architecture (which includes tools like Apache Spark and Titan Graph Database deployed on Google Cloud Platform).
