Recommendations at Videoland (2021)

Authors: Daan Odijk

With the largest commercial TV channels in the Netherlands, RTL
plays an important role in society. We reach over 85% of Dutch peo-
ple on a weekly basis, spending on average around 45 minutes a day
with us and our content. We feel a part of all of the Netherlands. We
actively pick up the challenge to show a diverse and representative
view in the media.
Videoland is our subscription-based video-on-demand (VOD)
platform, with over a million subscribers and thousands of local
and international titles. Our daily challenges on Videoland revolve
around supporting our users in discovering new content, within a
mix of editorial and personalized content.
In this talk, we detail the challenges and practical solutions in
building the recommender systems for Videoland. In part 1, we
discuss the tweaks and algorithms we deployed in this commercial
settings. We show their impact using offline evaluations and online
experiments with our users. In part 2, we discuss what we learned in
developing novel algorithms based on sequences of user behavior
