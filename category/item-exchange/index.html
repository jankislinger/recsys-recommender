A Recommendation-Based Book-Exchange System Without Using Wish Lists (2015)

Authors: Yiu-Kai Ng, Maria Soledad Pera

We introduce EasyEx, a recommendation-based book exchange system which identifies potential exchanges for a user solely based on the user’s item list. EasyEx is novel, since it effectively identifies books that are likely appealing to users, and optimizes book-exchange cycles to increase serendipity, instead of simply swapping books on users’ wish lists as performed by existing item-exchange algorithms. Experimental results have verified that EasyEx others users recommended books that satisfy their interests and contributes to the item-exchange mechanism with a new design methodology.
