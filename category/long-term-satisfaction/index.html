Reward innovation for long-term member satisfaction (2023)

Authors: Jiangwei Pan, Henry Wang, Gary Tang, Justin Basilico

Many large-scale recommender systems train on engagements because of their data abundance, immediacy of feedback, and correlation to user preferences. At Netflix and many digital products, engagement is an imperfect proxy to the overall goal of long-term user satisfaction. One way we address this misalignment is via reward innovation. In this paper, we provide a high-level description of the problem and motivate our approach.
