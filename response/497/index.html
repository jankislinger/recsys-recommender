Title: Time Slice Imputation for Personalized Goal-based Recommendation in Higher Education
Abstract:
Learners are often faced with the following scenario: given a goal for the future, and what they have learned in the past, what should they do now to best achieve their goal? We build on work utilizing deep learning to make inferences about how past actions correspond to future outcomes and enhance this work with a novel application of backpropagation to learn per-user optimized actions. We apply this technique to two datasets, one from a university setting in which courses can be recommended towards preparation for a target course, and one from massive open online courses (MOOCs) in which course pages can be recommended towards quiz preparation.
